Arguments:
    ./a3 [integrator] [stepSize] [vis_index]

optional parameters:
    [integrator] one of "e" "t" "r" "mr"
        "e": Euler
        "t": Trapezoidal
        "r": RK4
        "mr": My implementation of RK4

    [stepSize] float

    [vis_index] int, index of the particle in pendulum system, -1 displays nothing
